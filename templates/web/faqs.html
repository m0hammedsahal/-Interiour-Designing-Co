Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of interior design services, including residential and commercial design, space planning, project management, and more. Our goal is to bring your vision to life.

How much does your service cost?

Our pricing depends on the scope of the project. We offer customized packages based on your needs and budget. Contact us for a detailed quote.

What is the project timeline?

The project timeline varies depending on the size and complexity of the design. On average, a residential project may take between 6 to 12 weeks to complete.

Do you offer consultations?

Yes, we offer initial design consultations to discuss your vision, goals, and budget. This helps us create a tailored plan for your space.

Can I be involved in the design process?

Absolutely! We believe in collaboration and value your input throughout the design process. Your feedback helps us ensure the final design reflects your preferences.
